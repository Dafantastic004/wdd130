Overview

Purpose

This Website will give us the opportunity to connect with more potential customers who are intrested in rafting.And also to use our online presence to highlight the exciting rafting expriences er offer, share photos and videos, and provide details about trip packages and safty measures. To also make it easier for customers to make online booking and payment to plan and severve their rafting tips, and improve their experience.

Audience

Our water rafting website is primarily aimed at adventure enthusiasts and outdoor lovers seeking thrilling and memorable rafting experiences. Scenario Questions: Who are our potential customers? Potential customers include individuals aged 18-45 who enjoy outdoor activities and seek adrenaline-pumping adventures like white-water rafting. Families and groups of friends looking for exciting group outings also form a significant part of our target audience. What is the geographic location of our target audience? Our target audience is geographically diverse, including both local residents and tourists visiting our rafting location. We aim to attract adventure seekers from nearby cities and regions as well as those planning vacations in our area. What are the common interests and motivations of our target audience? Our target audience is motivated by the desire for adventure, the love of nature, and the pursuit of unique experiences. They are interested in activities that challenge them physically and mentally, and they value safety and professionalism in their outdoor adventures.
