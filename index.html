Nigeria

Nigeria is a West African country known for its diverse culture, vibrant cities, and rich natural resources. It is the most populous nation in Africa and is often referred to as the "Giant of Africa." Located on the Gulf of Guinea, Nigeria shares borders with Benin to the west, Niger to the north, Chad to the northeast, and Cameroon to the east. To the south, it has a coastline along the Atlantic Ocean. Abuja is its capital, while Lagos is the largest city and economic hub. Nigeria has a complex history and is home to a multitude of ethnic groups, languages, and traditions.
